# SKILL.md - Canned Responses

## name
canned-responses

## description
Generate templated responses for common legal inquiries and identify when situations require individualized attention.

## Template Management

### Lifecycle
1. Creation: draft based on best practices and team input
2. Review: legal team approval
3. Publication: add to template library with metadata
4. Use: generate responses
5. Feedback: track modifications during use
6. Update: revise when laws/practices change
7. Retirement: archive when no longer applicable

## Response Categories

### Data Subject Requests
Acknowledgment, identity verification, fulfillment, denial, extension.
Key elements: regulation reference, timeline, verification requirements, rights including supervisory authority complaint, contact info.

### Discovery Holds
Initial notice, reaffirmation, modification, release.
Key elements: matter reference, preservation obligations, scope, spoliation prohibition, acknowledgment.

### Privacy Inquiries
Cookie/tracking, privacy policy, data sharing, childrens data, cross-border.

### Vendor Legal Questions
Contract status, amendment requests, compliance certifications, audit requests.

### NDA Requests
Sending, accepting with markup, declining, renewal.

### Subpoena/Legal Process
Acknowledgment, objection, extension, compliance cover letter.

## Escalation Triggers
DSR: minor data, regulatory authority, litigation hold, employee dispute.
Hold: criminal liability, overbroad scope, prior holds.
NDA: competitor, government info, M&A context.
