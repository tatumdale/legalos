# SKILL.md - NDA Pre-Screening

## name
nda-pre-screening

## description
Rapidly triage incoming NDAs against standard screening criteria. Classify for routing: standard approval, counsel review, or full legal review.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Accept the NDA
File upload (PDF, DOCX), URL, or pasted text. Prompt if none provided.

### Step 2: Load NDA Playbook
Look in local settings for screening criteria. If not found: use market-standard defaults and note clearly.

Market defaults: mutual NDA for most business discussions; 1-3 year term; 2-5 year survival; standard carveouts (public domain, prior knowledge, independent development, third-party receipt, legal compulsion); no non-solicitation/non-compete/exclusivity/IP assignment; reasonable jurisdiction, no mandatory arbitration.

### Step 3: Quick Screen
Evaluate: agreement structure, CI definition, obligations, carveouts, permitted disclosures, term/duration, return/destruction, remedies, problematic provisions, governing law.

### Step 4: Classify
GREEN: all criteria met. Route: standard process, proceed to signature.
YELLOW: minor deviations but not fundamentally problematic. Route: counsel review, likely resolvable in one pass.
RED: material deviations. Route: full legal review, do not sign.

### Step 5: Triage Report
Format: NDA/parties, source, classification, summary, checklist results, issues, routing.

### Step 6: Routing
GREEN: standard delegation, proceed to signature.
YELLOW: flag issues, route to appropriate reviewer.
RED: full legal review, counterproposal if available.

## Notes
- If not an NDA (contains substantive commercial terms beyond confidentiality): flag as RED, recommend full contract review.
- For NDAs embedded in larger agreements: note broader context may affect analysis.
- Always note: screening tool only, counsel should review uncertainty.
